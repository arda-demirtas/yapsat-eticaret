# Business Rules

## User Roles

Guest
Customer
Admin

## Currency

USD

---

## Tax

Tax is calculated during checkout.

---

## Refund

Refund period: 14 days.

---

## Coupon

Coupons may be

- Percentage
- Fixed amount

Coupons cannot reduce the order below zero.

---

## Shipping

Free shipping above $100.

Otherwise shipping fee is calculated by destination.

---

## Notifications

Send email when

- Order placed
- Payment received
- Order shipped
- Order delivered
